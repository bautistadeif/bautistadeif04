Poem




In the tapestry of life, you're the thread that shines,
A radiant beacon, illuminating my heart's deepest lines.
Your love is a sanctuary, where I find solace and peace,
A haven where my soul can rest, and my heart can release.

Your touch ignites a fire that burns bright and true,
Melting the frost that once encased my heart, anew.
With every breath, I'm grateful for your loving grace,
A gift that fills my existence, with a sense of time and space.

In your eyes, I see a love that's pure and kind,
A reflection of the beauty, that you bring to my mind.
You're the melody that harmonizes my soul,
A symphony of love, that makes my heart whole.
